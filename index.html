All About Rhythm Rooms

We have the recording studio you need. Whether you’re a solo music producer, a full-scale rock band or even a string section, across our four rooms we can provide you with a world-class experience in studio recording. Maybe that’s why we’re often hailed as the best Recording Studio in London. Metropolis can arrange the ultimate creative experience. Harness the inspirational atmosphere created within our studios by utilising our brand new Writing Room, and be granted access to our private luxury accommodation situated on top of the Power House.

Some of the most important artists of all time have recorded and mixed albums at Metropolis Studios, including Queen, Michael Jackson, U2, Lady GaGa, Amy Winehouse, will.i.am, The Stone Roses, Lauryn Hill, The Verve, Adele, Rihanna and many more. However, we don’t only work with major labels, global icons and household names. We also cater for small and developing artists, and those with private projects. We can tailor packages to suit your needs – check our i-Mixing page for more details.
